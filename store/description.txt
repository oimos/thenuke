The Nuke — Instant Emergency Privacy Protection for Your Browser

Accidentally left personal tabs open before a screen share? Boss walking over while you're browsing Reddit? The Nuke closes targeted tabs, wipes their history, and camouflages your screen — all in under 200ms.

⚡ KEY FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🔑 One-Key Emergency Activation
Press Alt+Shift+X (customizable) to instantly trigger cleanup. No menus, no clicks, no delay.

🎯 Smart Domain Watchlist
Define which domains to monitor — social media, video streaming, news sites, and more. Built-in presets for quick setup.

🧹 Complete Trace Removal
Matching tabs are closed AND their history entries are deleted. Optional time-based history wipe (last 5, 15, 30, or 60 minutes).

🎭 Instant Camouflage
Choose between two cover modes:
  • Safe Redirect — Navigate your active tab to a professional site (LinkedIn, Google Docs, etc.)
  • Dashboard Overlay — Display a realistic Excel-style financial spreadsheet over your current page

📊 Professional Fake Dashboard
The overlay looks like an actual Excel workbook — complete with ribbon toolbar, formula bar, cell grid with financial data, sheet tabs, and status bar. Dismiss it anytime with ESC.

⚙️ Full Control
  • Manage your domain watchlist with add/remove and preset categories
  • Toggle between redirect and overlay camouflage modes
  • Set how much history to clear (panic level slider)
  • Test scan to preview which tabs would be affected — without closing anything
  • Enable/disable with a single toggle
